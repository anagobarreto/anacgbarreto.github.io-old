About

I come from the Pearl of Atlantic, also known as Madeira Island. I studied Visual Arts during High School as I always had a passion for art. When I finished school, I decided to move to London to look for a better future.

Since being in London I've been on customer support roles, but then realised it didn't interested me at all so I decided to expand my knowledge area. That was when I tried programming. I had a few friends in that area and it was super easy to ask for help or ask any questions relating to their jobs.

I went online to try to complete some free courses but just found them to be a bit tedious and non-progressive as I would get distracted whilst doing them. I was a bit thrown off on the idea of becoming a programmer as it felt that it would be a bit harder if I only relied on online resources.

After a couple of months, I decided to quit my job and prioritise my goals as I was totally unsatisfied on my job at the time and it became really monotonous. I wanted to learn new things everyday instead of felling like I was forgetting and becoming dumber. It was a harsh decision I must admit, and I'm shocked how I went with it.

My boyfriend suggested that I would go to a boot camp or an immersive course as it would prepare me well for the future in this industry. I was a bit put off as it would be high priced and I had quit my job a few weeks before. After thinking I decided to get some help as the Immersive course would definitely be an investment for my future and would change my career prospects.

Currently I'm waiting for the Web Development Immersive course to start in General Assembly as I've been granted a place. It'll start on the 20th March with the termination date on the 16th June. Meanwhile I'm learning as much as I can before the course so that I'm more comfortable.
